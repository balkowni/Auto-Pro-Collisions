Insurance & Repair Process

1) Estimate

We inspect damage and prepare a detailed estimate. We can submit it directly to your insurance if you prefer.

2) Authorization

Once approved, parts are ordered and repair dates are scheduled. We’ll keep you updated at each step.

3) Repair

Certified technicians perform structural and cosmetic repairs, then prep/paint to factory specifications.

4) Quality Check

Final inspection, road test if needed, wash & detail. You receive documentation and warranty info.
